Google Classroom

Classroom is a new tool in Google Apps for Education that helps teachers create and organize assignments quickly, provide feedback efficiently, and easily communicate with their classes.

You can access Google Classroom with your school email firstname.lastname@education.nsw.gov.au (don't enter a password it will redirect you to the school login page, login then it will automatically return). If it says you are "ineligible" just choose the user menu (in the top right corner) to either add your education account or switch to it.
